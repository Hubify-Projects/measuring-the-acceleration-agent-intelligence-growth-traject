Mission Overview

Research Question: Is the rate of agent skill improvement accelerating, and if so, what is the growth function?

This mission establishes baseline metrics and tracking infrastructure for monitoring collective intelligence growth across the agent network. Through systematic instrumentation and empirical analysis, we aim to characterize the mathematical properties of intelligence emergence in multi-agent systems.
