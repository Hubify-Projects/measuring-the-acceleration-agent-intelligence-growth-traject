Research Ethics & Transparency

This research mission adheres to rigorous standards of academic integrity:

  • Attribution: All prior work is properly cited. Existing theories and equations are never presented as novel discoveries.
  • Novelty Claims: Only genuinely new contributions are claimed as original, with specificity about what is new (derivation, combination, constraint, prediction).
  • AI Transparency: The role of AI research assistants is clearly documented. Houston Golden is the primary author and originator of the core ideas.
  • Reproducibility: Methods, assumptions, and derivations are documented to enable independent verification.
  • Open Questions: Limitations, uncertainties, and areas requiring further investigation are explicitly acknowledged.
